Harry L. Horning

1925 SAE President

Harry Horning was first noted as the designer of the structure and operating mechanism of the Duluth Steel Bridge. Horning founded the Waukesha Motor Company in 1906. He is also credited with helping the Society of Tractor Engineers and the National Gas Engine Association merge into SAE International.
